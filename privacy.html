Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

MyCosmicFate ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website mycosmicfate.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when taking our quiz, including birth date, birth time, birth location, and your responses to personality questions. This information is used solely to generate your personalised cosmic reading.

We do not require account registration. We do not collect your name or email address unless you choose to provide it for a paid report.

How We Use Your Information

Your quiz responses are used to generate a personalised reading. They are not stored permanently on our servers beyond the duration of your session unless you purchase a report. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Facebook Integration

Our site offers an optional feature allowing you to share content on Facebook in exchange for accessing a free reading. When you use this feature, you interact directly with Facebook's platform. We do not collect or store your Facebook credentials, profile information, or post data. Any sharing activity is governed by Facebook's own Privacy Policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy.

Payments

Paid reports are processed via Stripe. We do not store your payment card details. All transactions are handled securely by Stripe in accordance with PCI-DSS standards.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to remember your quiz session and whether you have already unlocked your reading. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

We use AI technology to generate personalised readings. Your quiz responses may be processed by this service to produce your result. No personally identifiable information beyond your quiz answers is transmitted.

Data Retention

Session data is cleared when you close your browser. Paid report data is retained only as long as necessary to deliver your report and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at: hello@mycosmicfate.com

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@mycosmicfate.com
